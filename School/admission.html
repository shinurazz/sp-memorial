2022-23 APPLICATION FORMS

ADMISSIONS FOR THE 2022-23 ACADEMIC YEAR ARE NOW OPEN!

Admission will be granted based on the information provided by the parents / guardian of the candidate as well as the performance of the candidate in the aptitude / entrance test. While submitting the admission application, the parents / guardian need to submit few statutory documents as listed:

Documents required:

  • 4 recent passport size colour photograph.
  • Copy of the Birth Certificate (issued by the Municipal Corporation only).
  • Copy of the mark sheet of the last examination issued by the previous school.
  • For students seeking admission to XI Standard, copy of mark sheet of X Standard issued by the respective Board along with the Migration Certificate should be provided.
  • For students seeking admission to VI Standard and above, Character Certificate need to be produced.
  • Transfer Certificate / School Leaving Certificate (in original) issued by the school the candidate last attended must be submitted within 15 days of his / her joining the school.
  • Details of Local Guardian providing name, relationship with student, address, contact number, FAX number, email id.
